Getting Started

This tracker transforms Phasmophobia into an RPG progression system. Your investigator earns experience, gold, talent points, equipment upgrades, and permanent progression outside the game.

Before You Begin

  • For the best experience, have all Tier 3 equipment unlocked in Phasmophobia. If you don't, use the highest tier currently available until you unlock it in-game.
  • Create a brand new empty loadout in Phasmophobia.
  • Equip one of every Tier 1 primary item.
  • Do not equip any secondary equipment.
  • Start a new character in this tracker.

Playing the Campaign

  1. Play a contract in Phasmophobia.
  2. Click Complete Contract.
  3. Enter the map, difficulty, reward money, and contract results.
  4. Click Roll Loot.
  5. Apply any equipment unlocks or upgrades to your Phasmophobia loadout before your next contract.
  6. Spend Gold in the Shop, earn Talent Points from leveling, and continue progressing your character.

Goal

Grow your investigator from a rookie with only Tier 1 primary gear into a veteran with fully upgraded equipment, completed talent trees, and mastery over every difficulty.
